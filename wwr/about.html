History

Our history starts in Bahia, Brazil, when our family visited an incredible place called "Chapada Diamantina National Park". The park has huge canyons with rivers of brownish waters and several waterfalls, best trekking destination in the country and rocky climbing in Lençois town.

The place still offers more adventure in its hundreds of caves, where prehistoric rock art can be found. The cities and villages attract thousands of tourists with their local culture, and beatiful landscapes. With so many types of adventures, why not rafting? Tha's the origin of our company!
